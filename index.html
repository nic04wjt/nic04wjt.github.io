    Projects

    Antibacterial Effects of Chinese Herbal Medicine

    Investigated antibacterial properties of Taraxacum officinale (dandelion) extracts against E. coli. Designed and executed experiments using zone of inhibition tests and performed statistical analysis to validate results.

    Tools: Excel.

    Pesticides and Radish Metabolism

    Explored the effects of lambda-cyhalothrin, a common pesticide, on radish peroxidase activity. Applied enzyme kinetics and spectrophotometry to measure oxygen production from peroxide decomposition. Analyzed trends and visualized data to evaluate pesticide impact on metabolic pathways.

    Tools: Excel, Logger Pro, Vernier Spectrophotometer.

    Mapping Jupiter

    Tracked Jupiter's motion and visualized its trajectory using Python and astronomical data. Leveraged data analysis and plotting techniques to create a detailed orbital map.

    Tools: Python, Jupyter Notebook, Matplotlib, Astropy.

    Genetic Inheritance Pattern Analyzer

    Designed and implemented a Python-based tool that leverages propositional logic and Boolean satisfiability (SAT) solving to analyze genetic inheritance patterns in pedigrees. Developed algorithms and recursive logic to validate pedigree structures and identify dominant/recessive or autosomal/X-linked traits. Ensured reproducible results through containerized development with Docker.

    Tools: Python, Docker.
